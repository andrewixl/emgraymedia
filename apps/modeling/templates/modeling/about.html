Who Am I?
My name is Emily Gray.

  • I am a Body Positive Model who loves photography as well as modeling, looking for opprotunity and gaining experience!
  • I love taking both Portrait and Travel Shots whenever and wherever I can. I love working with a wide range of models to promote diversity and all body types!
  • I currently live in the Greater Seattle Area and would be very excited to model or take photos of you!

What is Being Body Positive? It'll blow your mind.

I consider myself to be a body positive model. I strive to be body positivemodel because I believe it is important for everyone to be accepting of their body, and I hope to inspire some to do the same as me. I strongly promote self love and acceptance of everybody!

What Camera Do I Use? Canon EOS Rebel T6

Do I Edit My Pictures?
Yes, But I Do Not Manipulate

As both a photographer and a model, I do not support photo manipulation. I believe we should be accepting of all body types, colors, and sizes. I am a body positive model and in being one, I do not manipulate my photos. When I edit, I focus on the colors, the contrast, the background, in order to emphasize the model in the photo.
